About MultiDock Screening Tool

The MultiDock Screening Tool is an advanced graphical user interface (GUI) designed to facilitate high-throughput virtual screening using AutoDock Vina. It offers an intuitive interface for researchers to easily manage, execute, and analyze docking simulations.

Core Features

  • Seamless integration with AutoDock Vina for molecular docking.
  • Batch processing of multiple ligands and receptors.
  • Interactive visualization of docking results.
  • Customizable scoring functions and analysis tools.
  • User-friendly interface with advanced settings for expert users.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower researchers in the field of computational chemistry and drug discovery by providing a robust and user-friendly tool that accelerates the drug discovery process. The MultiDock Screening Tool is designed to streamline workflows, reduce time, and enhance the accuracy of virtual screening projects.

Why Choose MultiDock?

MultiDock combines the power of AutoDock Vina with an intuitive GUI, making it accessible to both novice and experienced users. With its advanced features and flexibility, MultiDock is the ideal tool for researchers seeking to conduct efficient and effective virtual screening.
